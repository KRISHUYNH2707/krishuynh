About me

Kris is a detail-oriented and results-driven data analyst, specializing in descriptive and predictive analytics. He graduated with a Distinction degree in Business Information Systems at Federation University Australia.

His strength is the ability to perform data pre-processing, analysis, and visualization. He possesses great analytical skills, excellent multitasking skills, strong attention to detail, and significant ability to work in a team. He is also keen on pursuing his career path through continuous learning and self-development.

  • Python, Javascript, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, GIT, Front-end Frameworks
  • Web Scraping, Data Wrangling, Data Analytics
  • Data Visualisation, Data Telling, Machine Learning
